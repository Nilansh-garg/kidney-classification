Detection Classes

What We Detect

Our model is trained to differentiate between four critical kidney conditions with clinical-level accuracy.

Normal

Healthy kidney tissue with no signs of pathological conditions. No intervention required.

Cyst

Fluid-filled sacs on or in the kidney. Usually benign but may require monitoring or treatment.

Stone

Hard mineral deposits formed inside the kidney. Can cause pain and urinary complications.

Tumor

Abnormal tissue growths that may be benign or malignant. Requires immediate medical attention.
