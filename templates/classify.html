Tips for Best Results

  • Use axial CT scan images
  • Ensure the kidney is clearly visible
  • Use high resolution images (min 224×224px)
  • Avoid blurry or overexposed scans
  • Grayscale or contrast-enhanced images work best

Privacy & Security

  • Images processed locally on-server
  • No data stored permanently
  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS)
  • HIPAA-aligned data handling
  • No third-party data sharing

Classification Classes

  • Normal — healthy kidney
  • Cyst — fluid-filled sac
  • Stone — mineral deposits
  • Tumor — abnormal growth
